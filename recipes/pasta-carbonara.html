Pasta Carbonara

Description

Pasta Carbonara is a Roman classic, the secret of which is that there is no mention of cream. The velvety texture of the sauce comes from eggs, cheese and cooking water.

This recipe contains 2 servings.

Ingredients

  • Sphagetti 200g-250g
  • Guanciale 100g-150g
  • 1 Whole egg and 2 yolks
  • Parmesan 1dl
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
