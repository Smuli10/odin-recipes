Macaroni Casserole

Descriotion

Macaroni casserole is the king of Finnish home cooking. Here's a traditional,

easy and guaranteed recipe that will give you a succulent result.

This recipe contains 6 servings.

Ingredients

  • Macaron 400g
  • Minced meat 400g
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Beef stock cube
  • Milk 7dl
  • 3 Eggs
  • Spices of your choice
