Neapolitan Pizza


Description

Neapolitan pizza is an art in its simplicity. The secret is not in the complicated ingredients,

but in the time and heat. Here is a home-cooked version that will bring you very close to the authentic atmosphere.

This recipe makes four pizzas


Ingredients

Base

  • Water 3dl
  • Yeast 2g
  • Flour about 500g
  • Salt 2tl

Topping

  • 1 can of Mutti pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella 250g
  • A handful of basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

Base

Dough preparation

  1. Dissolve the yeast in the water Add about half of the flour and mix until smooth
  2. Add salt and the remaining flour gradually.
  3. Knead thoroughly: If you knead by hand, do it for at least 10–15 minutes, until the dough is elastic and does not stick to your hands
  4. Cover the bowl with a cloth and let the dough rest at room temperature for 2 hours

Balling

  1. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and divide it into 4 parts
  2. Roll the pieces into tight, smooth balls
  3. Place the balls in a floured bowl (leave room to spread), cover, and let rise at room temperature for another 4–6 hours
