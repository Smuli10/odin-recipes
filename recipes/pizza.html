Homemade neapolitan Pizza


Description

Neapolitan pizza is an art in its simplicity. The secret is not in the complicated ingredients,

but in the time and heat. Here is a home-cooked version that will bring you very close to the authentic atmosphere.

This recipe makes four pizzas


Ingredients

Base

  • Water 3dl
  • Yeast 2g
  • Flour about 500g
  • Salt 2tl

Topping

  • 1 can of Mutti pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella 250g
  • A handful of basil
  • A drizzle of olive oil

Steps

Base

Dough preparation

  1. Dissolve the yeast in the water Add about half of the flour and mix until smooth
  2. Add salt and the remaining flour gradually.
  3. Knead thoroughly: If you knead by hand, do it for at least 10–15 minutes, until the dough is elastic and does not stick to your hands
  4. Cover the bowl with a cloth and let the dough rest at room temperature for 2 hours

Balling

  1. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and divide it into 4 parts
  2. Roll the pieces into tight, smooth balls
  3. Place the balls in a floured bowl (leave room to spread), cover, and let rise at room temperature for another 4–6 hours

Shaping (Don't use a rolling pin!)

  1. Take the ball and press it with your fingertips from the center outwards. Leave about 1–2 cm of "buffer" around the edges, which you do not press into a mound
  2. Gently stretch the dough with your hands until it is thin

Topping

  1. Tomatosauce: Place about 1.5 tablespoons of sauce in the middle of the dough. Spread it with the back of a spoon in a spiral motion from the middle towards the edges. Leave about 2 cm of space (cornicione) around the edges so that they can rise in the oven until they are fluffy
  2. Mozzarella: Sprinkle the cheese evenly over the sauce
  3. Basil: Put basil after baking the pizza
  4. Olive oil: Finish the pizza with a drizzle of olive oil. The traditional way is to pour a thin strip of oil in the shape of a hexagon (a small circle and a line) on the pizza
